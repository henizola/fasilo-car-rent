About Us

Sileshi Tefera Car Rent is a family business, founded by Silishi Tefera in Bahir Dar (Amhara State) of Crete the year 2011, with business license number 1/65/8/03 aiming at providing high quality services in the rental of cars for short & long term periods, for public, Non-governmental, and private organizations.

Our company started with just 2 cars & in a very few number of customers; however, in a very short time we expanded rapidly and reach the number of cars more than 15 and established a large customer basis.
